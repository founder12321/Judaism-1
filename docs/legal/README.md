## Key Legal Positioning

Judaism 1 provides a **technology marketplace** connecting users with independent pastoral consultants. Judaism 1:

- Does **not** provide therapy, medical advice, or mental health treatment
- Does **not** employ providers as therapists
- Does **not** guarantee halachic rulings — providers offer guidance within their movement's norms
- Requires providers to refer clinical cases to licensed professionals

## BetterHelp FTC Lesson

Do **not** share intake questionnaire data (mental health-adjacent, religious, life circumstances) with advertising pixels (Facebook, Google, etc.). Use first-party analytics only until attorney approves otherwise.
