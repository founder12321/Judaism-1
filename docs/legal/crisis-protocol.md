# Crisis Protocol — Judaism 1

> **DRAFT — NOT LEGAL ADVICE.** Have a licensed attorney review before implementation.

## Purpose

Judaism 1 is not a crisis service. This protocol ensures users in acute distress are directed to appropriate resources and booking is paused when needed.

## Triggers

Block or pause booking and show crisis resources when intake form or free text indicates:

- Suicidal ideation or self-harm intent
- Active abuse requiring immediate intervention
- Psychotic episode or severe mental health crisis language

**Keyword examples (non-exhaustive):** "kill myself," "end my life," "suicide," "self-harm," "want to die"

## User-Facing Response

Display immediately (do not proceed to payment):

---

**We're concerned about your safety.**

Judaism 1 provides pastoral guidance, not emergency or crisis support. If you or someone you know is in crisis:

- **Call or text 988** — Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (US)
- **Text HOME to 741741** — Crisis Text Line
- **Call 911** if you are in immediate danger

When you're safe, we're here for pastoral guidance. Please consider reaching out to a licensed mental health professional for ongoing support.

## Provider Training

All Guides must acknowledge:

1. How to recognize when a user needs clinical referral
2. Script for suggesting licensed therapy without abandoning the user
3. When to break confidentiality (imminent harm)
4. Never attempt to treat active suicidal ideation via pastoral session alone

## Platform Actions

- Log crisis trigger events (no detailed intake stored in analytics)
- Do not retarget crisis users with marketing
- Admin review of flagged submissions within 24 hours
