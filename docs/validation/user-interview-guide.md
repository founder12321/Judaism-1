# User Demand Validation — Interview Guide

Use this guide to interview 10 potential users. Track responses in `user-interview-tracker.csv`.

## Goal

Validate pain points and willingness to pay $75–100 for a 45-minute session with a vetted rabbi or scholar.

## Target Segments (10 total)

| Segment | Count | Examples |
|---------|-------|----------|
| Unaffiliated / no regular rabbi | 4 | High Holiday Jews, remote workers, recent grads |
| Affiliated wanting more | 3 | Synagogue members, busy pulpit rabbi's congregants |
| Converts / Jews-by-choice | 2 | In conversion process or recently converted |
| Interfaith families | 1 | Partner learning about Jewish home life |

## Script (20 minutes)

### Opening (2 min)

> "I'm researching whether Jews would use a platform to book private 1-on-1 guidance with a vetted rabbi — for life questions, Jewish practice, or spiritual direction. Not therapy. Can I ask about your experience?"

### Core questions

1. **Current access:** Do you have a rabbi you can call with personal questions? When did you last reach out?
2. **Gap:** Think of a time you wished you had rabbinic guidance but didn't. What was it?
3. **Alternatives:** What did you do instead? (Google, friend, therapist, nothing?)
4. **Platform appeal:** Would you book a video session with a vetted rabbi matched to your denomination? Why or why not?
5. **Price sensitivity:** Would you pay $75 for 45 minutes? $100? $150? What's too much?
6. **Trust:** What would make you trust a provider on the platform? (ordination, reviews, denomination filter)
7. **Topics:** Which topics would you actually book? (marriage, parenting, grief, kashrut, conversion, career)
8. **Privacy:** Would you use this if your synagogue rabbi might find out? Does privacy matter?
9. **Frequency:** One-time or recurring? How often?
10. **Beta:** Would you join a free/discounted beta with 2–3 founding providers?

### Closing

> "Can I add you to our beta waitlist? No spam — just an invite when we launch."

## Success Criteria

- **7+ of 10** describe a real, recent unmet need
- **5+ of 10** say they would pay $75–100 for the right match
- **3+ beta signups** from interviews
- Clear **top 3 booking categories** emerge across interviews
