# Provider Supply Validation — Interview Guide

Use this guide to interview 5 rabbis/scholars before launch. Track responses in `supplier-interview-tracker.csv`.

## Goal

Confirm willingness to join Judaism 1 at an 80/20 revenue split, acceptable session rates ($75–150), and clear boundaries around pastoral vs. clinical work.

## Target Interviewees (5)


| #   | Profile                         | Denomination | Status |
| --- | ------------------------------- | ------------ | ------ |
| 1   | Pulpit rabbi, 5+ years          | Conservative |        |
| 2   | Pulpit rabbi, 5+ years          | Reform       |        |
| 3   | Orthodox rabbi or Torah scholar | Orthodox     |        |
| 4   | Chaplain (Neshama-certified)    | Any          |        |
| 5   | Torah educator (no smichah)     | Pluralistic  |        |


## Script (30 minutes)

### Opening (2 min)

> "I'm building Judaism 1 — a marketplace where Jews can book 1-on-1 pastoral guidance with vetted rabbis and scholars. This is not therapy; it's Torah-informed life counsel. I'd love your honest feedback."

### Core questions

1. **Pain / fit:** Do you get requests from people who aren't in your congregation? How often?
2. **Interest:** Would you offer 2–4 sessions per month on a platform like this? Why or why not?
3. **Pricing:** What would you charge for a 45-minute session? ($75 / $100 / $125 / $150+)
4. **Revenue split:** At an 80/20 split (you keep 80%), does that work? What split would you need?
5. **Boundaries:** Where do you draw the line between pastoral guidance and therapy? When do you refer out?
6. **Denomination:** How important is it that users match to your movement's norms?
7. **Vetting:** What credentials should the platform require? What would make you trust other providers listed?
8. **Logistics:** Preferred video tool (Zoom, Google Meet)? Typical availability (evenings, Sundays)?
9. **Concerns:** What would stop you from joining? (liability, synagogue politics, brand, etc.)
10. **Referrals:** Know 2–3 colleagues who might be interested?

### Closing

> "If we launch a founding provider program with manual vetting and featured placement, would you be open to a follow-up conversation?"

## Success Criteria

- **3+ of 5** express strong or moderate interest
- **Average target rate** falls within $75–150 for 45 min
- **No show-stoppers** on liability that legal templates can't address
- **At least 2 warm referrals** to other providers

## Red Flags

- Expects platform to guarantee halachic authority for all users
- Unwilling to sign independent contractor agreement or refer clinical cases
- Wants to diagnose or treat mental health through the platform
